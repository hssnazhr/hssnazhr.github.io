EV Vehicle Sales Impact on Oil Displacement

This personal project analyzes global electric vehicle sales and their impact on oil displacement, showcasing the trends and predictions for future market behavior. China's comprehensive and multi-faceted policies, including mandates, financial incentives, infrastructure development, and industrial support, have collectively driven the rapid adoption of EVs. These policies are central to reducing the country's reliance on oil for transportation, leading to significant oil displacement and setting an example for other nations looking to transition to cleaner energy sources.
